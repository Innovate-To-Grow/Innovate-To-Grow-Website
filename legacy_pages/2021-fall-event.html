Fall 2021 - Event

Friday, December 17, 2021 - 10:30AM - 4:00PM

  • 10:30  Registration + Coffee - Conference Center 
  • 11:00  Expo (Posters - Demos) - Conference Center
  • 12:45  Presentations - Campus and Zoom (see schedule for Rooms)
  • 15:00  Awards Ceremony - Conference Center 
  • 15:30  Reception - Conference Center
Register NOW !

Preparing for the Event

  • Register ASAP  to attend in person or on zoom!
  • To attend on Zoom, ensure your account displays your Full Name.
  • Review schedule, projects, and teams (below): check for updates!
  • You may click on a team (e.g. CAP14) to open that team info.
  • Then, you may click the open/close icon to view project details.
For Attendees
For Judges

Attend in Person:

  • Mask required (provided if needed).
  • Park in the reserved area in the Bellevue Lot (follow signs).
  • Walk or shuttle to the Conference Center.
  • Pick up your badge at the Registration desk.
  • Registration and coffee start at 10:30.
  • Expo doors open at 11:00 for student posters/demos.
  • Lunch is served in boxes at the Conference Center.
  • Presentations start promptly at 12:45! 
  • Search the room of your desired track (see the schedule below).
  • You may also attend the Award Ceremony and the Reception.

Attend on Zoom:

  • You may only view the Presentations, not the Expo and Awards.
  • Each Track is held in a separate Zoom Room.
  • Plan to click the I2G Home Page by 12:40 to find and join a track!
  • Access to Zoom rooms will appear in the Schedule below and in the I2G Home Page on the event day after 12:30!
  • Sign in your Zoom account, make sure your “Display Name” is your Full Name.
  • Click the button of the desired Track # (passcode is:  I2G).
  • Presentations will start promptly at 12:45!  
  • You may change Zoom Room to change track!
