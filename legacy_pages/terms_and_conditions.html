I2G Privacy Policy

Effective Date: Tuesday, January 28, 2025

Innovate to Grow (I2G) at UC Merced is committed to respecting and protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal data. By using our services or providing us with your personal information, you agree to the practices outlined in this policy.

1. Information We Collect

We collect personal information when you interact with our services. The types of information we collect include:

  • Personal Information: We collect your name, phone number, email address, and event-related information when you register for I2G events or services.
  • Automatically Collected Data: We may collect data about your interaction with our website and communications, such as usage statistics, and information about your browser or device.
  • Text Message Interaction: If you consent to receive text messages from us, we collect data related to the messages you send or receive, such as delivery confirmations, replies (e.g., STOP, HELP), and usage statistics.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your personal information is used to provide services to you, including:

  • Event Registration: To process your registration for events, confirm your participation, and provide necessary updates.
  • Two-Factor Authentication (2FA): To verify your identity when you register for events via SMS messages, if you consent to receiving such messages.
  • Event Reminders and Updates: If you opt in to receive SMS messages, we will send you event updates, including ticket confirmations, QR codes, and reminders related to your participation.
  • Customer Support: To respond to your inquiries, including resolving issues with registration or event-related questions. You may contact us via email or text for support.
  • Legal and Operational Requirements: To comply with applicable laws or to protect our rights, prevent fraud, or resolve disputes.

3. Your Consent for Receiving Text Messages

We respect your communication preferences, especially regarding text messages. By providing your phone number and opting in, you consent to receive SMS messages from us related to event registration, authentication, and other necessary communications for events. You can choose to receive SMS messages at any time when you opt-in to participate in events.

You have full control over the messages you receive:

  • If you no longer wish to receive text messages, you can stop them by replying "STOP" to any message you receive from us. You will receive a confirmation message acknowledging your request to unsubscribe. After this, no further messages will be sent to you unless you re-opt in.
  • If you have questions or need assistance with our SMS service, you can reply with "HELP" to receive help, or contact us directly via email at i2g@ucmerced.edu.

4. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes. However, we may share your information in the following situations:

  • Service Providers: We work with third-party providers, such as Twilio, to send SMS messages. These service providers will have access to the necessary information to send messages on our behalf but are prohibited from using your data for any other purposes.
  • Legal Compliance: We may be required to disclose your information if required by law, regulation, or legal process to protect our rights or respond to lawful requests from government authorities.

5. Data Retention

We retain your personal information only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy, including compliance with legal obligations and operational requirements. Once your data is no longer required, we will delete it in a secure manner.

6. Your Rights

As a user, you have certain rights over your personal data:

  • Opt-Out: You may opt out of receiving SMS messages at any time by replying "STOP." We will stop sending you messages, and you will not receive further communication unless you choose to opt in again.
  • Access and Update: You can request access to your personal data and ask us to correct or update it. Please contact us via email to make these requests.
  • Data Deletion: You may request the deletion of your personal data, subject to applicable legal or operational obligations.

7. Security

We take reasonable precautions to protect your personal data from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure. However, no method of electronic transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee the security of your data.

8. Children's Privacy

Our services are not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe we have collected information from a child, please contact us immediately.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or your personal data, please contact us at:

  • Email: i2g@ucmerced.edu

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or to comply with legal and regulatory requirements. We will post the updated policy with the "Effective Date" clearly indicated at the top of the page.

I2G Messaging Terms and Conditions

Program Name: I2G Event Messaging

1. Program Description: By opting in, you consent to receive SMS messages related to the I2G event, including event reminders, ticket information, authentication codes, and other relevant updates. The frequency of messages may vary, and you will receive 3-4 messages during the semester you registered for.

2. Opt-Out: You can cancel the SMS service at any time by texting "STOP" to the short code. Upon doing so, you will receive a confirmation message, and you will no longer receive SMS messages from us unless you opt in again.

3. Customer Support: If you are experiencing issues with the messaging program, you can reply with "HELP" for assistance or email us directly at i2g@ucmerced.edu.

4. Message Delivery: Carriers are not liable for delayed or undelivered messages.

5. Message and Data Rates: Message and data rates may apply for any messages sent from you to us or from us to you. Please check with your wireless provider for more information about your plan's rates. You will receive 3-4 messages during the semester you registered for.

6. Privacy: For more details about how we collect, use, and protect your personal data, please review our Privacy Policy above.
