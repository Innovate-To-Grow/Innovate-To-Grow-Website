2021 Spring Event

Friday, May 14, 2021 at 11:00AM - 2:00PM

We will feature our student presentations online via Zoom: 

  • Presentations will start promptly at 11:00!
  • Plan to click the I2G Home Page by 10:50 to find and join a track!
  • Access to Zoom rooms will appear in the Schedule below and in the I2G Home Page on the event day.
Register NOW !

Preparing for the Event

  • Register ASAP!
  • Check if your Zoom account displays your Full Name.
  • If you do not have a Zoom account: sign up for Zoom.
  • You may review schedule, projects, and teams (being updated).
  • You may review detailed information:
For Attendees
For Judges

Interactive Schedule (below)

  • Each Track is held in a separate Zoom Room.
  • You may view the time of each team's presentation.
  • You may click on a team (e.g. CAP14) to open that team info.
  • Then, you may click the open/close icon to view project details.
  • You can click Track # (on event day) to access the desired Track.

The Day of the event:

  • Sign in your Zoom account.
  • Click the button of the desired Track #.
  • Make sure your “Display Name” is your Full Name.
  • Enter the Passcode (I2G2021).
  • Welcome to the I2G presentations!
